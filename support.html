Marketing Support Materials

Marketing materials are available to promote the Wiley Digital Archives acquisition in your library.

We encourage you to promote the Wiley Digital Archives collection(s) that you have acquired for your library. Ready-to-use and customizable promotional materials are available to alert your library patrons of their newly accessible resource. This will help increase awareness and usage of the collection(s). Promote your Wiley Digital Archives collection(s) within your library, on your library portal, through email, on social media and more. You can get creative and even create lawn signs for around campus! These materials are available for the overall platform and for each collection. They are available for you to download, print, customize and share as needed.

Please contact us if you would like to receive physical copies of any of these items and we will discuss your requirements.
